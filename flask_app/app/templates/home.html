Welcome!

This is a web app that allows visualisation of archeological research data

This website provides an interface to enable interactive visualisation of mass spectrometry data collected for archeological research purposes. In order to jump straight into visualising the data head over to the visualisations page. If you would like to learn more about what the visualisation is showing and where and why the data was collected then head over to the Visualisation Details and Research pages.
